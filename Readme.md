# 📊 Apple Global Sales Dashboard (Tableau)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing Apple’s global sales data to understand **revenue trends, product performance, and regional distribution**.

The dashboard transforms raw data into **actionable insights** for business decision-making.

---

## 🔗 Live Dashboard

👉 https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/aman.baikar/viz/AppleGlobalsalesDataset/AppleExecutiveGlobalSalesDashboard

*(Interactive – click on regions and charts to explore detailed insights)*

---

## 📊 Dataset

* Dataset: Apple Global Sales
* Key fields:

  * `Order Date` – Date of sale
  * `Category` – Product category (Mac, iPhone, iPad, etc.)
  * `Revenue` – Sales revenue
  * `Region/Country` – Geographic location

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Revenue Trend Over Time

* Line chart showing monthly revenue
* Helps identify patterns and fluctuations

---

### 2. Revenue by Product Category

* Bar chart comparing revenue across product categories
* Highlights top-performing products

---

### 3. Global Sales Distribution

* Map visualization showing revenue across regions
* Helps identify key markets

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **Mac is the highest revenue-generating category**, outperforming other products
* Revenue trends show **fluctuations over time**, indicating demand variation
* Certain regions contribute significantly more, highlighting **key revenue markets**

---

## 🧠 Interactivity (Key Feature)

* Users can **click on regions in the map** to filter the entire dashboard
* Allows deeper analysis of country-level performance
* Enhances usability for business stakeholders

---

## 💡 Business Use Case

* Track product performance across categories
* Identify high-performing regions
* Analyze revenue trends for decision-making
